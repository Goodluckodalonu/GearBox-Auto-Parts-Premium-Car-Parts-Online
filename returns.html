Policy Summary

At a Glance

  • Return Window: 30 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Unused, in original packaging with all accessories
  • Return Shipping: Free for defective/wrong items, customer-paid for change of mind
  • Refund Method: Original payment method or store credit

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Your Return

Start your return within 30 days of delivery by contacting our returns department or using our online portal.

Required Information:

  • Order number
  • Item(s) you wish to return
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the item (for damaged/defective returns)
Email Returns Call: (555) 123-4569
2

Package Your Item

Proper packaging ensures your return is processed quickly and without issues.

Do Include

  • Original packaging and boxes
  • All accessories and manuals
  • Return authorization form
  • Protective padding for fragile items

Don't Include

  • Personal notes or extra items
  • Tape directly on product packaging
  • Used consumables (fluids, filters)
  • Items without RMA number
3

Ship Your Return

Once you receive your Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) and shipping label, send your package back to us.

Shipping Instructions:

For Free Returns (Defective/Wrong Items):

We'll email you a prepaid shipping label. Print it and attach to your package. Drop off at any UPS or FedEx location.

For Customer-Paid Returns (Change of Mind):

You'll receive an RMA number. Ship using any carrier and insure the package for its full value. Send to our returns address provided in your RMA email.

Important: Always include your RMA number inside the package. Returns without RMA numbers may experience significant delays.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund.

Refund Type Processing Time How It Works Notes
Credit Card Refund 5-10 business days Refunded to original payment method Most Common
PayPal Refund 2-5 business days Refunded to your PayPal account Instant in some cases
Store Credit 24-48 hours Added to your GearBox account +10% Bonus
Check Refund 7-14 business days Mailed to billing address For certain payment methods only

You'll receive an email notification when your refund is issued. The timing of when funds appear in your account depends on your financial institution.

Return Conditions & Eligibility

Item Condition Requirements

To be eligible for a return, your item must meet the following conditions:

Acceptable Condition

Unused and in original condition
Original packaging intact
All tags, labels, and accessories included
No signs of installation or use

Not Acceptable

Installed or used items
Damaged or altered packaging
Missing accessories or parts
Items purchased more than 30 days ago

Special Cases & Exceptions

Defective or Damaged Items

If you receive a defective or damaged item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a free return and replacement.

  • Provide photos of the damage/defect
  • Keep all original packaging
  • Do not attempt to install or repair

Wrong Item Shipped

If you receive the wrong item, contact us immediately. We'll provide a prepaid return label and expedite the correct item.

  • Check part numbers against your order
  • Do not open if it's clearly the wrong item
  • We'll provide overnight shipping for the correct part

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned for safety or hygienic reasons:

  • Opened fluids (oil, coolant, brake fluid)
  • Used filters (oil, air, fuel)
  • Custom or special-order parts
  • Electrical items with cut/tampered wires
  • Software or digital products
  • Clearance or final sale items

Exchanges

Need a Different Size or Part?

We make exchanges easy. If you need a different size or compatible part, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our returns department with your order number
  2. Specify which item you want to exchange and for what
  3. Our team will verify compatibility and availability
  4. We'll process an exchange order and provide return instructions
  5. Once we receive your return, we'll ship the replacement

Pro Tip: Contact our technical support team at (555) 123-4568 before exchanging to ensure you get the right part for your vehicle.

Standard Exchange

  • Processed after we receive your return
  • No additional shipping charges if same value
  • Standard shipping time for replacement

Advanced Exchange

  • We ship replacement immediately
  • Requires credit card authorization hold
  • Overnight shipping available

Contact returns department to request advanced exchange.

Avoiding Restocking Fees

To avoid restocking fees:

  • Verify vehicle compatibility before ordering using our VIN lookup tool
  • Contact technical support for installation questions
  • Return items in original, unopened condition
  • Initiate returns within 30 days of delivery

Restocking fees help cover the costs of inspecting, restocking, and potentially remarketing returned items.

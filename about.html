About GearBox

Premium auto parts for enthusiasts and professionals since 2020

Our Story

GearBox was founded in 2024 by a team of automotive engineers and racing enthusiasts who were frustrated with the lack of quality parts available to everyday car lovers. What started as a small garage operation has grown into a leading online destination for premium auto parts.

Our mission is simple: to provide car enthusiasts, professional mechanics, and DIYers with access to high-performance parts that don't break the bank. We believe everyone deserves to experience the thrill of a well-tuned machine.

From our headquarters in Detroit, the heart of American automotive innovation, we're committed to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in aftermarket parts.

Shipping Restrictions

Restricted Items

  • Hazardous materials (certain fluids, batteries)
  • Oversized items (some body panels, bumpers)
  • International restrictions may apply for certain parts

Special Handling

  • Fragile items marked with special packaging
  • Heavy items (engine blocks, transmissions) may have additional shipping fees
  • Temperature-sensitive items shipped with climate control

Delivery Issues & Support

Damaged or Lost Packages

If your package arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery (or expected delivery date for lost packages). We'll work with the carrier to resolve the issue and send replacements as needed.

Report an Issue

Delivery Delays

While we make every effort to deliver orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to weather, carrier issues, or high order volumes. You'll be notified if your order is significantly delayed.

Delivery estimates are not guaranteed during holiday seasons or severe weather events.

Address Changes

Need to change your shipping address? Contact us immediately if your order hasn't shipped yet. Once an order has shipped, address changes may not be possible and could result in additional fees.

Request Address Change

Orders & Payments

Placing an order is simple:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click the cart icon and review your items
  3. Click "Checkout" and enter your shipping information
  4. Choose your shipping method and enter payment details
  5. Review your order and click "Place Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation email immediately. If you don't see it, check your spam folder.

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Shop Pay: Installment payments available

All payments are processed securely through encrypted channels. We don't store your full credit card information on our servers.

You can modify or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it, as long as it hasn't entered the shipping process.

  • To cancel: Contact our customer service immediately at support@gearboxparts.com or call (555) 123-4567
  • To modify: We may be able to add items or change shipping methods before shipment

Once an order has shipped, you'll need to wait for delivery and use our return process.

Shipping & Delivery

Shipping times vary based on your location and chosen shipping method:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days (Free on orders over $100)
  • Express Shipping: 2 business days - $19.99
  • Overnight Shipping: 1 business day - $34.99
  • Local Pickup: Same day (Detroit store) - Free

Orders typically process within 1-2 business days before shipping. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Currently, we only ship to the United States (all 50 states) and Canada. For Canadian orders:

  • Delivery takes 7-14 business days
  • Additional customs fees and taxes may apply
  • Some restricted items cannot be shipped internationally

For shipping to other countries, please contact us for a custom quote and availability.

You can track your order in several ways:

  1. Email: Check your order confirmation email for tracking links
  2. Account: Log into your GearBox account and view order history
  3. Carrier Website: Use the tracking number provided on the carrier's website (UPS, FedEx, USPS)
  4. Contact Us: Call our support team at (555) 123-4567 for assistance

Tracking information is typically available within 24 hours of your order shipping.

Returns & Exchanges

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery:

  • Unused Items: Full refund if returned in original packaging with all accessories
  • Defective Items: Free return shipping and full refund or replacement
  • Change of Mind: Return shipping paid by customer, 15% restocking fee may apply
  • Opened/Used Items: Subject to inspection, partial refund may be issued

To initiate a return, contact returns@gearboxparts.com with your order number.

To exchange an item:

  1. Contact our returns department within 30 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order number and reason for exchange
  3. We'll email you a return authorization and shipping label
  4. Once we receive the returned item, we'll ship the replacement

Note: For size or fit exchanges, we recommend contacting our technical support first at (555) 123-4568 to ensure you get the correct part.

Refund processing times:

  • Upon Receipt: Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the return
  • Credit Card: 5-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • PayPal: 2-5 business days
  • Store Credit: Instant upon approval

You'll receive an email notification once your refund has been processed. The timing of when the funds appear in your account depends on your financial institution.

Products & Compatibility

We offer several ways to ensure part compatibility:

  • Vehicle Selector: Use the vehicle selector tool on product pages
  • Fitment Information: Check the "Specifications" tab on each product
  • VIN Lookup: Enter your Vehicle Identification Number for exact matches
  • Live Chat: Chat with our technicians for compatibility questions

Pro Tip: Always double-check fitment by comparing your old part numbers with our product specifications. When in doubt, contact our technical support.

We carry both OEM-equivalent and performance aftermarket parts:

  • OEM Equivalent: Meets or exceeds original equipment specifications
  • Performance Parts: Upgraded components for enhanced performance
  • Brand Selection: We carefully select reputable manufacturers
  • Quality Assurance: All parts undergo quality checks before shipping

Each product page clearly indicates whether it's OEM-equivalent or performance-grade. We also provide manufacturer warranty information for every part.

Yes, most parts come with manufacturer warranties:

  • Standard Parts: Typically 1-year limited warranty
  • Performance Parts: Varies by manufacturer (often 2-5 years)
  • Lifetime Warranty: Available on select brands
  • GearBox Guarantee: 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all purchases

Warranty details are listed on each product page. To make a warranty claim, contact our support team with your order number and photos of the issue.
